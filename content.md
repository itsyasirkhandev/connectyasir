### The Challenge

```

wanted to run paid ads. One problem — he had
nowhere to send the traffic.

No website. No landing page. No logo. Nothing.

a full professional site: landing page with
8–9 sections, plus privacy, terms, contact,
about, application tracking, and success
stories pages.

On top of that, he needed a backend system —
a dashboard where he could see and manage
every loan application that came through the
site. Without calling a developer every time
someone filled out a form.

No brand. No website. No technical team.
He needed everything — from one person.
```

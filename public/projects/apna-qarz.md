# Apna Qarz Case Study

**Finance Advisory Website & Lead Generation**

From no website to a full platform with 1,000+ leads in the first month.

## Project Overview

- **Project Type:** Full-Stack · Done-for-You
- **Industry:** Finance & Advisory

## The Challenge

Before we started, Apna Qarz had a zero digital footprint. They possessed no brand identity, no automated lead capture systems, and relied entirely on manual outreach to generate business.

## The Solution & Aftermath

We built a blazing fast custom platform from scratch. This included a secure admin dashboard for managing their data. The result was highly successful, generating 1,000+ high-intent leads in the first 30 days.

## Technologies Used

- Next.js
- Supabase
- Tailwind CSS
- Vercel
